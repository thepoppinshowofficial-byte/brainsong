The science behind The Brain Song

The program is built around two key neuroscience concepts that are documented in peer-reviewed research.

Gamma Brainwave Entrainment

Gamma frequencies (~40 Hz) are associated with high-level cognitive function, focus and memory consolidation. MIT's Picower Institute has published research showing gamma entrainment effects in animal models of Alzheimer's disease.

BDNF — Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor

BDNF is a protein that supports the growth and maintenance of neurons — sometimes called "fertilizer for the brain." The audio claims to stimulate natural BDNF production through gamma frequency exposure.

Frequency Following Response

A well-documented phenomenon where the brain's electrical activity synchronises with rhythmic external stimulation. This is the mechanism behind all brainwave entrainment technology and is supported by decades of neuroscience research.
